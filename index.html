Portal for Sketchy Gamers

Welcome to the totally legit gamer portal — handcrafted in the shadows using expired code and pure chaos. Inside you'll find weird little games, questionable design choices, and maybe a hidden malware boss battle (just kidding). I'm not saying playing here will improve your gaming skills, but it will confuse your browser history. Enter if you dare. Or if you're just really bored. Thanks for stopping by — your sanity is non-refundable. Now click something random and hope for the best.



Available Sketchy Games

  • The Clicking Game

    Click the click-me button as many times as you can before the timer runs out!

    Be careful though, it will jump all over the place. So hang onto your mouse, click fast, and try your best. Average player scores around 16 points. Good Luck! Don't let your mouse fling.

  • Guess The Number

    Try to guess the secret number in as few attempts as possible!

    With every guess, you'll get a hint — higher or lower. Sounds easy, right? But don’t get too confident... the number is sneaky. A good guesser usually nails it under 5 tries. Can you beat that? Focus up and trust your gut!

  • Rock Paper Scissor

    Pick your move — rock, paper, or scissors — and face off against the computer!

    It's a classic showdown of reflexes and luck. First to 5 wins takes the crown. The computer might be fast, but can it outsmart you? Most players win… eventually. Keep calm, think ahead, and throw your best shot!
